======================================================================
DESIGN GUIDELINES
======================================================================

Style:
- Clean, minimal, lots of white space
- No shadows or very subtle ones
- No gradients (or very subtle)
- Modern sans-serif font (already in project: Inter or similar)

Colors (use Tailwind):
- Background: white (#ffffff) or very light gray (#fafafa)
- Primary text: gray-900
- Secondary text: gray-500
- CTA button: black bg + white text (or dark primary color)
- Accent: keep minimal

Layout:
- Max width: max-w-6xl centered
- Sections: py-20 or py-24
- Mobile responsive (Tailwind responsive prefixes)
