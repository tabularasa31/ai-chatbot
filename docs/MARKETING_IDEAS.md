## Key messages

### "Your support mate" — main metaphor and positioning

Not "AI chatbot". Not "widget". But **support mate** — alive, active, there 24/7.

- Answers user questions around the clock.
- Sends a report every morning about what happened.
- Tells you where it lacks knowledge.
- Learns from feedback.

**Key phrases:**
- Tagline: **"Your support mate, always on."**
- Landing headline: **"Meet your new support mate."**
- Hero subheadline: "Works 24/7. Sends you a daily report. Gets better every week."
- LinkedIn: "I added a new support mate to our team. They work 24/7, never complain, and send me a report every morning."

**Why "mate" is better than "team member":**
- More alive, friendlier, memorable.
- Not corporate.
- Rhymes with `getchat9.live` — live = alive, nearby.

**Why this works as positioning:**
- Removes fear "bot will replace people" → bot helps people.
- Creates emotional connection with the product.
- Differentiates us from dry "AI chatbot platform" competitors (DocsBot, SiteGPT).
- Daily report makes Chat9 "visible" every day — even when client doesn't open the dashboard.
